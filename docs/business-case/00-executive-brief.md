# Executive Brief

## Business Context

A regional express logistics company needs better visibility into international shipment performance, operational bottlenecks, service incidents, customer satisfaction, and cost per move. The operations team needs a structured way to identify where service quality is at risk and which corrective actions should be prioritized.

## Problem Statement

How can the operations team identify the main drivers of shipment delays and prioritize corrective actions to improve service quality and customer satisfaction?

## Project Objective

- Monitor delivery and SLA performance.
- Identify service levels, regions, checkpoints, and facilities with delay exposure.
- Classify operational incidents and root causes.
- Evaluate how delays affect CSI.
- Recommend corrective actions based on evidence.

## Completed Deliverables

- Cleaned shipment dataset.
- Relational Access database.
- SQL investigation queries.
- Power BI dashboard.
- Business recommendations.

The completed implementation demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow: Power Query ETL, Microsoft Access relational modeling and SQL, a Power BI semantic model, measures, and a four-page executive dashboard.
