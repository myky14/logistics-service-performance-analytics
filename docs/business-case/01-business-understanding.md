# Business Understanding

A regional express logistics company wants to improve service quality across international shipments. The operations team needs better visibility into shipment delays, checkpoint compliance, root causes, customer satisfaction, and cost per move.

This project simulates the work of a Service Quality Analyst using Excel, Power Query, Microsoft Access, SQL, and Power BI. The completed workflow helps a logistics operations team monitor delivery performance, investigate operational bottlenecks, and prioritize corrective actions.

The source dataset originally comes from a public supply chain dataset. For this project, selected fields were cleaned, engineered, and reframed into an express logistics analytics scenario for learning and portfolio purposes.

This is a simulated analytics case and does not claim to use real courier company data.

## Business Objective

The objective is not only to present a dashboard, but to demonstrate a structured analytical workflow from business understanding, data preparation, data modeling, SQL investigation, visualization, and operational recommendations.

The final implementation includes Power Query ETL, Microsoft Access SQL, a relational database, a Power BI semantic model, a measures table, and a four-page dashboard covering Executive Overview, Operational Bottlenecks, Root Cause Analysis & Customer Impact, and Operational Priorities & Data Quality.
