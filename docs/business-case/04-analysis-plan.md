# Analysis Plan

This plan connects business questions to the analytical data model, implemented SQL investigation, and final Power BI dashboard.

| Business Question | Hypothesis | Data Tables | SQL / Metric Direction | Power BI Visual | Expected Business Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| What is the overall shipment performance? | Overall shipment performance may reveal service quality risk. | `tbl_Shipments`, `tbl_SLA_Targets` | Count shipments, calculate on-time rate, transit delay, and SLA compliance. | KPI cards and performance trend | Establish a baseline for service quality and determine whether performance requires operational attention. |
| Which service levels or regions have the highest delay exposure? | Delay exposure may be concentrated in specific service levels or regions. | `tbl_Shipments`, `tbl_SLA_Targets` | Group delay metrics by service level and destination region. | Matrix or clustered bar chart | Prioritize service lanes or regions for deeper investigation and corrective action. |
| Which operational checkpoint contributes most to shipment delay? | Gateway checkpoint may be the primary operational bottleneck. | `tbl_Checkpoints`, `tbl_Shipments` | Compare Origin-to-Gateway and Gateway-to-Destination checkpoint segment timing. | Segment duration bar chart | Identify which operational transition should receive process improvement focus. |
| What are the most common incident issue buckets? | Gateway congestion and customs delay may be the dominant incident drivers. | `tbl_Incidents`, `tbl_Shipments` | Count incidents by issue bucket and compare against delayed shipments. | Ranked bar chart | Focus root-cause analysis on the issue categories most associated with service failures. |
| Which origin groups or regions should be prioritized for corrective action? | Certain origin groups or regions may require prioritized corrective action. | `tbl_Shipments`, `tbl_Incidents`, `tbl_Checkpoints` | Compare delay, incident, and checkpoint segment patterns by origin group and Destination Region. | Operational Priorities table | Direct improvement efforts toward the groups or regions with the highest service quality risk. |
| How does transit delay affect CSI score? | Longer transit delays may reduce CSI scores. | `tbl_Shipments`, `tbl_CSI_Scores` | Compare CSI score patterns across transit delay bands. | Scatter plot or line chart | Show how operational delays may affect customer experience and escalation priority. |
| How does cost per move vary by service level? | Cost per move may vary significantly by service level. | `tbl_Shipments` | Calculate shipment cost per move and compare by service level. | Column chart | Support service mix and cost-performance discussions without claiming final cost optimization results. |
