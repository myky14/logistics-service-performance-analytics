# Problem Framing

## Problem Statement

How can the operations team identify the main drivers of shipment delays and prioritize corrective actions to improve service quality and customer satisfaction?

## 5W1H

| Dimension | Framing |
|---|---|
| Why | The company needs to protect service quality, reduce avoidable service failures, and improve customer satisfaction. |
| Who | Operations management, service quality analysts, gateway teams, origin facilities, customer-facing teams, and customers. |
| What | The operations team has observed shipment delays, inconsistent checkpoint performance, and service incidents that require structured root-cause analysis. |
| Where | Across destination regions, service levels, origin facilities, and operational checkpoints. |
| When | Throughout shipment movement, especially around gateway transfer and destination milestones. |
| How | Delays and weak checkpoint compliance reduce customer visibility, increase escalation risk, and may lower CSI scores. |
